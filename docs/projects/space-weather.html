Space Weather Visualization

Description

Long-running research program focused on understanding and visualizing the impact of solar storms on terrestrial infrastructure, particularly power grids. This umbrella project has produced both the Global-GMDs tool (published in Elsevier SoftwareX) and ongoing work on the 2003 Halloween Storm (under revision at AGU Space Weather).

Key contributions include developing visualization models that map geomagnetic disturbances globally and determining the spatial coverage of magnetometer observatories around the world to assess monitoring gaps during extreme events.

Key Highlights

  • Ongoing since April 2022 — multiple publications and presentations.
  • Peer-reviewed publication in Elsevier SoftwareX (Global-GMDs).
  • Paper under revision at AGU Space Weather (Halloween Storm).
  • Presentation at GEM 2024 Workshop, Colorado, USA.
  • SuperMAG R API adopted by ~4,000 researchers worldwide.
